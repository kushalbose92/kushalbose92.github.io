Our organisation **Neulearn.ai** is dedicated to performing research beyond the Large Language Models. We believe artificial general intelligence or AGI can be achieved by going beyond LLMs. Our primary target is to design learning algorithms and intelligent systems that possess the true characteristics of human-level cognition.
